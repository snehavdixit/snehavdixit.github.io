I am currently a graduate student studying High Energy Physics at the University of Nebraska, Lincoln.

On 4 July 2012, the ATLAS and CMS experiments at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) at CERN, Geneva, jointly announced the discovery of the Higgs Boson. This particle is the last 'missing piece' in the Standard Model of Particle Physics - the most successful theory describing the basic building blocks of our universe. Yet, several important questions in physics remain unanswered. My current research is a part of the effort to find the answers to these questions at the LHC.
